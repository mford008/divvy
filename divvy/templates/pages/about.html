Sharing Made Easy.

Jane moves into a new apartment building but doesn't have all the appliances/household items she needs. She logs into this app to find out who in her apartment building has a vacuum for her to borrow. She joins the ‘group’ for her apartment building and finds that one of her new neighbors has one listed that is available to borrow. She submits a borrow request, and a time frame that works for them both. Jane borrows the vacuum, returns it shortly after, and everyone is happy! A few weeks later, she lists her power drill as available for anyone in her complex to borrow.
